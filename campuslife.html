My Campus Life

I am so grateful to have met my roommates, Khairina, Fatin, Rabiatul, and Solehah. They have made my life so much brighter with their kindness, support, and laughter. Living together has created beautiful memories, from sharing meals and stories to helping each other through tough times. Each of them holds a special place in my heart, and I’m thankful for the bond we’ve built. They’re more than just roommates—they’re my second home.

I’m so thankful for my classmates who became my besties. At first, we were just sharing notes, group projects, and laughs during class, but over time, we became so much closer. They’re the ones I can rely on, not just for school but for everything in life. We’ve shared so many unforgettable moments, from late-night study sessions to deep talks and fun outings. They’ve turned the ordinary days into extraordinary memories, and I couldn’t ask for better friends. From classmates to besties, they’ve truly become an irreplaceable part of my life.

Joining Sekretariat Mahasiswa Anak Perak (SEMARAK) has been a transformative experience for me. It’s more than just an organization; it’s a community that has enriched my life in so many ways. Through SEMARAK, I’ve gained valuable knowledge about leadership, teamwork, and collaboration. I’ve had the chance to take part in meaningful events, tackle new challenges, and contribute to projects that truly make an impact. The experiences I’ve gained have helped me grow both personally and professionally, making me more confident and skilled. SEMARAK has also introduced me to inspiring individuals who continuously motivate me to do better. I am incredibly grateful for the opportunities, lessons, and memories this journey has brought into my life.
