My Campus Life

I am so grateful to have met my roommates, Khairina, Fatin, Rabiatul, and Solehah. They have made my life so much brighter with their kindness, support, and laughter. Living together has created beautiful memories, from sharing meals and stories to helping each other through tough times. They’re more than just roommates—they’re my second home.

I’m so thankful for my classmates who became my besties. At first, we were just sharing notes, group projects, and laughs during class, but over time, we became so much closer. They’ve turned the ordinary days into extraordinary memories, and I couldn’t ask for better friends.

Joining Sekretariat Mahasiswa Anak Perak (SEMARAK) has been a transformative experience for me. Through SEMARAK, I’ve gained valuable knowledge about leadership, teamwork, and collaboration. This organization has been an essential part of my growth.
